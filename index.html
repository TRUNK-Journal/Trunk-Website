What we do

TRUNK serves as a vibrant platform for undergraduate STEM students at the University of Toronto to share their research, ideas, and discoveries. By publishing original work, we celebrate student achievement, foster academic growth, and strengthen connections across the universityâ€™s vibrant scientific community.

Showcasing Innovation

Highlights original research and creative solutions developed by undergraduate students, fostering a culture of innovation and discovery within the university community.

Student-Led Platform

Operated by course student unions, the journal empowers students to take leadership roles in academic publishing, peer review, and editorial processes.

Academic Recognition

Provides a formal platform for undergraduates to publish their work, gain recognition, and build their academic portfolios for future opportunities.

Building Community

Connects students across STEM disciplines, encouraging collaboration, networking, and the sharing of ideas within and beyond the university.

Expanding Impact

Disseminates student research to a wider audience, increasing the visibility and impact of undergraduate contributions to science and technology.

Peer Support & Mentorship

Facilitates mentorship and peer review, helping students develop critical skills in scientific communication and constructive feedback.

Who we are

TRUNK is a collaborative effort run by various student unions at the University of Toronto. Our mission is to provide a platform for undergraduate students to publish their research and creative work, fostering a culture of academic excellence and innovation.

Our Current Team

The Astronomy Student Union, Chemistry Student Union, Mathematics Student Union, Physics Student Union, and the Computer Science Student Union.
